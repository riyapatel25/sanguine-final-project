    The Cova Project

    The Cova project stems from the recognition that poverty, isolation, and discrimination leave menstruators without the necessary sanitary products, taking away from school/work. As these menstruators already struggle to provide food for their families, this project aims to releive them of the financial/physical burden of menstruation products through menstruation cups!

    • Helping Women Period

    The Helping Women Period is an organization which focuses on providing no cost menstrual necessities to those experiencing homeless/low-income disparity and other challenges. Through their volunteer and donation services, menstruators will not be held back by a lack of resources.

    • The Pad Project

    The Pad Project aims to end the cultural stigma around periods and to empower menstruators. This project spreads awarness on mentrual health, funds the placement of pad machines, and runs menstual hygiene workshops.
